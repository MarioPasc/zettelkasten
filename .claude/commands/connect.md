3. Prioritise cross-domain connections (e.g., a maths concept relevant to an ML method).
4. Present suggestions grouped by strength:
   - **Strong**: clearly related, should definitely be linked.
   - **Interesting**: conceptual overlap worth considering.
   - **Speculative**: a stretch, but potentially illuminating.
